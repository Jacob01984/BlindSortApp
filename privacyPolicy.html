Privacy Policy for Blind Number Sort App

1. Introduction

Our app, Blind Number Sort, respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

2. The Data We Collect About You

The app collects non-identifiable gameplay data to send to Apple Game Center for achievements/ leaderboards. We do not store any of this data, but it is sent to these Apple services (Game Center) for storage and use. We also use UserDefaults to store game settings and progress locally on your device. We do not collect any personal or sensitive data about you.

3. How We Use Your Data

We do not collect personal or sensitive data about you for our use. The non-identifiable gameplay data collected by the app is sent to Apple's Game Center for the purpose of enabling game features such as achievements and leaderboards. This data is not used by us for any other purpose.

This privacy policy was last updated on July 12, 2023.
